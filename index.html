About Me

Hi! I'm Matthew, a 13-year-old dog lover from Austin, Texas. I have three amazing dogs of my own, and I absolutely love spending time with them. Whether it's taking them for a walk around the neighborhood or playing fetch in the park, I make sure they have a great time and get the exercise they need.

I started this dog sitting service to help other dog owners in my community. I understand how busy life can get, and sometimes it's hard to find time to give your furry friends the attention they deserve. That's where I come in! I'll treat your dogs as if they were my own, ensuring they are happy, healthy, and well-cared for.

I'm responsible, reliable, and passionate about dogs. I look forward to meeting you and your pets!
